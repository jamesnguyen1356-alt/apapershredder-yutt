​Privacy Policy

**1. Hello!**

We care about your privacy. This Privacy Policy is here to tell you how we collect, use, share, and keep your info safe when you use our website our app, or any of our stuff (we’ll just call these our Services).

By using our Services, you're saying it's okay for us to handle your info as described here.

**2. What Info We Grab**

We collect info so we can make our Services better for you! This info falls into two groups:

   **a. Info You Give Us**

   You can share info with us directly, such as:

   *   Your name
   *   Your email
   *   Your phone number
   *   Payment or billing info
   *   Where we should ship stuff (if needed)
   *   Your login info
   *   Any messages or feedback you send

   **b. Info We Snag Automatically**

    When you use our Services, we might automatically get things like:

   *   Your IP address and general location
   *   What type of device you’re using, your browser, and your operating system
   *   Which pages you visited and how long you stayed
   *   The website you came from
   *   Cookies and how you use our site

Cookies? They help us see what's popular, remember your settings, and generally make things better. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

**3. How We Use Your Info**

   We use your info to:

   *   Keep our Services up and running and make them better
   *   Process your orders and manage your account
   *   Talk to you (answer questions, get back to you about feedback)
   *   Send you updates, deals, or other fun stuff (if you say it's okay)
   *   Make our website work better and be easier to use
   *   Protect against scams, security risks, or anything illegal
   *   Follow the law

**4. (For People in Europe and the UK)**

If you're in Europe, here's why we can use your info:

   *   You said it's cool (Consent).
   *   We need it to give you our Services (Contract).
   *   The law says we have to (Legal obligation).
   *   It helps us run our business, and it doesn't mess with your privacy (Legitimate interests).

**5. Who We Share Your Info With**

   We don't sell your personal info!

   We only share it if:

   *   We have service people to process payments, send emails, etc.
   *   We're doing something with a partner (like a special deal).
   *   The law tells us to.
   *   Our company gets bought or merges with another company (we'll let you know).

   Everyone we share with has to keep your data safe and only use it how we say.

**6. How Long We Keep Your Info**

   We only hold onto your info as long as we need it to provide our Services, follow the law, or sort out any problems. When we don't need it anymore, we delete it or scramble it so it's anonymous.

**7. Your Rights**

   Depending on where you live, you might have the right to:

   *   See what info we have about you
   *   Ask us to fix or delete your info
   *   Tell us to stop using your info
   *   Take back your permission
   *   Get a copy of your data


   We might need to check who you are before we do anything.

**8. Keeping Your Data Secure**

   We do our best to keep your personal info safe with things like encryption, secure servers, and access controls.

   But, no internet thingy is 100% secure.

**9. When Your Data Goes Abroad**

   Your info might be sent and stored outside of your country. If it is, we'll make sure it's handled safely and by this Policy.

**10. Kids' Privacy**

   Our Services are not for kids under 13 (or 16 in some places).

   We don't knowingly collect info from kids. If we find out we have a kid's info, we'll delete it ASAP.

**11. Links to Other Sites**

   We might have links to other websites or apps. We're not in charge of their privacy stuff.

   Check out their privacy policies if you visit them!

**12. Changes to This Policy**

   We might update this Privacy Policy now and then.

   We'll post any changes on this page with a new date. If they're big changes, we might email you or put a notice on our site.
